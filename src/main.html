faq

How does it work?

Our clients don’t pay any upfront fees. It’s a simple "no win — no fee" setup.

What if I can't visit your office?

We're happy to provide you with a free consultation at your place or the hospital if you can’t visit us.

Who will I talk to if I call your office?

When you give us a call, you'll be directly connected to one of our specialists. They will then link you with a dedicated injury attorney, ensuring you receive the maximum compensation for your case.

When may I contact you?

We are available 24/7. Our attorneys take on all the hassle by offering comprehensive on-site support, conducting investigations, collecting information, and obtaining police reports.

What if my insurance company deems me at fault?

Take a moment and reach out to us. Allow our attorneys to examine the evidence and work towards securing a favorable settlement for you.

What if I just need advice?

Contact us at 929-551-9617 for free legal advice on your case

How much is my case worth?

Determining the worth of your case requires a thorough evaluation, and while we can't provide an immediate answer, we will begin the investigation. Insurance companies and other parties aim to cut costs. We advise reaching out to us first for expert guidance.

What types of personal injury cases can you help with?

Our attorneys specialize in a wide variety of cases, ranging from motorcycle, bus, and train accidents to bicycle, car, taxi, Uber or Lyft incidents, truck collisions, commercial vehicle mishaps, pedestrian incidents, workplace injuries, hit-and-run, slip-and-fall accidents, birth trauma, spinal cord injuries, medical malpractice, and construction accidents.
